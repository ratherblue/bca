Jason Street

LEUCADIA, CALIFORNIA

Status: Site Sold. See Commercial tab for current project, Dos Palmas Mexican Bakery.

Specs: Mixed-use, four 2 bedroom residential units above commercial space.

Commentary: This mixed-use project comprised four unique loft-style residential units located above grade-level commercial space. Designed with “funky Leucadia” in mind, two warehouse style buildings surround a lower scale roadside commercial building that looks like it may have been built in the 1940’s.
